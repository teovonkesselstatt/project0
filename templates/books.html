Theo von Kesselstatt

Books

As a kid, my parents induced me into reading every day. I used to spend the minutes or hours of everyday reading my stories for kids, eventually "My First Larousse" became my favourite type of books, or the argentine comic "Gaturro", that I read back and forth uncountable times. Somewhere along my adolescence I lost this habit, but I continued to enjoy my Literature classes, where I came to read amazing books like the Iliad, and other classics. Later on, in the same Literature classes I came upon my actual favorite author, Borges, and I became passionate about his work. But I did not recover my reading habit until January 1st, 2019. Ever since, I read everyday, and can no longer imagine not reading anymore. Here is a list of my 10 favorite books, not in any particular order.
